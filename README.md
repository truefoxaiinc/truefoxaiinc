Truefox AI Inc builds secure, scalable and production-ready AI agents, intelligent surveillance systems, computer vision platforms and enterprise automation software.

We transform operational challenges into intelligent, maintainable and production-ready software systems.

## What we build

We design artificial-intelligence products and enterprise software for customer engagement, operational automation, visual intelligence and secure business workflows.

### AI agents

Our work includes AI voice agents, customer-support and sales agents, quotation and document-generation systems, and business workflow automation designed for production environments.

### Computer vision

We build computer-vision platforms for face, person, vehicle and object detection, people counting, occupancy analytics and real-time video processing.

### Intelligent security surveillance

Our surveillance engineering work includes IP-camera monitoring, intelligent event detection, video analytics and operational tools for reviewing and responding to security events.

### Enterprise software

We develop enterprise web and mobile applications, SaaS products, APIs, cloud platforms and DevOps systems that integrate reliably with existing business operations.
